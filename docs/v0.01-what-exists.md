## What you can do right now (the felt experience)

Open the link and walk the scroll. It's the full six-chamber SoulSeed Compass:

1. **Threshold** — REZZIE notices you ("You look like you're between worlds"), time-aware. Optional name.
2. **Identity Seed** — the Between-Place gate: four tappable cards (*I lost something / I'm building something / I'm becoming someone / I don't know yet*). Your choice becomes your arrival vector.
3. **Present State**, **4. Memory Root**, **5. Trajectory Branch** — reflective questions; REZZIE mirrors what you say.
6. **Living Invitation** — COACH hands you your **SoulSeed Snapshot** (identity signal · present state · returning pattern · emerging trajectory · first invitation) and your permanent **HURL**. "This isn't a link. It's a continuation."

Then the part that matters: **leave, come back through your HURL, and it remembers you.** It doesn't say "welcome back" — REZZIE says *"Last time, you were [building]. What changed?"*, you answer, and your Snapshot **evolves** from what it already knew plus what's new.

It's also fully styled — the HOLOTORUS coherence field breathing behind the scroll, glass/gold/dark, the breathing paw, REZZIE's words typed out, cardiac scene transitions.

## What's real under the hood (HOLO Core — the actual asset)

The scroll is a thin skin. The value is the brain behind it, and it's all built and live:

- **One permanent identity + HURL per person**, anonymous-first — no signup to start; email is captured optionally at the end and merges cleanly if they later log in from another device.
- **The Memory Lattice** — every meaningful moment is recorded as an event; insights become memories, scoped (profile / state / narrative / trajectory / …). The Snapshot is **derived from stored memory, not freshly invented** — run it twice, you get the same shape. The story stays downstream of the data.
- **Orchestration** — the chamber flow is *data* (a manifest), never hardcoded. Adding/reordering chambers, or a whole new product, is a data change.
- **HURL minting** — a deterministic coordinate of where someone is.
- **One brain, many doors** — a second product can already read the same person's memory (proven). Build the brain once, reuse everywhere.
- The frontend **never touches the database** — everything goes through one typed client (the SDK) over versioned Core endpoints. This is what makes the next product a thin shell, not a rebuild.
